Mordern Technology Overview

Modern technology allows machines to become more and more complex, and manual-control tasks can mostly be automated. Modern airplanes are popular examples of this. There is a certain irony in such automation which should not be overlooked. On the one hand automation serves to avoid human errors and to reduce mental load, but on the other hand there are emergency situations in which the system changes from automatic to manual control.
